Welcome!

Jian (Colin) Zhang is an Assistant Professor of Finance at the HKU Business School. His work has been published or accepted in the Journal of Financial Economics, Management Science, the Review of Economics and Statistics, the Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, and the Review of Finance. He is also an Associate Editor of the Journal of Business Research.

Research interests include:

  • Household Finance
  • Real Estate
  • Behavioral Finance
  • Investments
  • Sustainability
